BidOcean Design System

Foundation tokens and core components, built directly from the brand logo. Two brand colors anchor the system — the red mark and the plum wordmark — with an ocean-blue functional accent. All ramps are HCT-derived. Click any swatch to copy its hex.

Foundations

Color
From your logo. The mark is #FF2A13 (red) and the wordmark is #311331 (plum) — so the system is genuinely two-color. Plum becomes the primary text/ink and a secondary brand color; the neutral grays carry a subtle plum tint so everything feels related. The #3B3734 mentioned earlier doesn't appear in the logo. Note: the SVG mark fills as #FF2912, a near-identical variant — standardize on #FF2A13.

Primary — brand red (HCT hue 28°). #FF2A13 is the brand swatch (500); use 600 for solid fills, 700 for hover so white text passes WCAG AA.

Secondary — brand plum from the wordmark (HCT hue 332°). For dark surfaces, secondary buttons, headings, and accents. #311331 is the ink anchor.

Accent — ocean blue (HCT hue 255°). Functional: links, focus rings, info, charts, progress.

Neutral — plum-tinted gray. 700–900 for text and dark UI, 50–200 for surfaces and borders.
